About Me

I love coding and solving complex puzzles, and my goal is to apply my passion for programming to advance the causes that I believe in. Through working in reproductive health research, I honed my skills in data management/analysis and programming more broadly. I have built a full-stack clone of Tumblr using Ruby on Rails, postgreSQL, and React/Redux. I made a turn-based game using Javascript and HTML5 Canvas.
